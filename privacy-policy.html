Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Habit Tracker-Kickoff ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information when you use our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

Habit Tracker-Kickoff does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. All habit data you create (habit names, completions, streaks, notes) is stored exclusively on your device using a local database and is never sent to any server.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain information to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable, non-personal device identifier)
  • Approximate location (country/region level, derived from IP address)
  • Device information (OS version, screen size, language)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their policies. You can review Google's privacy practices here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalised ads at any time by going to your device's Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (Android 12+) or by enabling Opt out of Ads Personalisation on older versions.

3. Data Storage

All app data (habits, settings, profile) is stored locally on your device using Android's Room database and DataStore. We do not have access to this data. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data.

4. Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device using Android WorkManager. No notification data is transmitted externally.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
